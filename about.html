Our principal instructor, Oksana Skira, is a concert pianist and recording artist. She received her music education at the Music Collegiate in Drohobych, Ukraine. She holds an Honors Piano major at the Lviv State Musical Academy M.V. Lysenko in Lviv, Ukraine where she obtained her Master's degree in piano performance.

She currently holds the Certificate of Competency and Qualifications as a concert pianist, chamber music ensemble soloist, concertmaster, accompanist, and instructor. In addition, she's fluent in English, Ukrainian, Polish, and Russian.
