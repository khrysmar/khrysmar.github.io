Lessons at Khrysmar

For students of all levels and needs

We're located in South Etobicoke, and teach all over the Greater Toronto Area.

Enjoy piano lessons in your home or in ours! Our lessons will prepare you for RCM exams, recitals, auditions, competitions, and will help you reach your musical goals.

Play for yourself, play for others, and discover the joy of music in highly personalized private lessons. We offer 30 minute, 45 minute, and 60 minute lessons depending on your ability and preference. For more details including a quote for your lesson you could reach us at khrysmar777@gmail.com or call (416)-877-2902.

To showcase their progress, students will have the opportunity to perform in front of an audience at our annual recital in June. Every year, students of all ages are invited to perform for their parents, friends, and peers, and will be able to pre-order a DVD of the performances to watch later! Join us afterward for coffee, tea, and light snacks.
